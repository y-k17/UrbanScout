About UrbanScout

UrbanScout utilizes cutting-edge technology to analyze satellite imagery for urban planning and development purposes. Our platform offers features such as land cover classification, object detection, and more, providing valuable insights to urban planners, developers, and policymakers.

Key Features

Land Cover Classification

Automated classification of land cover types for detailed analysis of urban landscapes.

Object Detection

Detection of urban infrastructure objects such as bridges, freeways, roundabouts, and intersections.

Actionable Insights

Providing actionable recommendations for urban planning and development based on analysis results.
